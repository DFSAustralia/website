GLOSSARY
* Next Match: details of next premiership season matchup
* Last 5: individual fantasy scores over the last 5 rounds
* Averages: season averages or form averages over last 3, 10, 20 games (excluding games with less than 50% TOG)
* Last 20 Games: standard deviation, max and min fantasy scores over last 20 games (excluding games with less than 50% TOG)
* Splits: averages over last 5 games vs opponent, at ground, in wins/losses, at day/night (excluding games with less than 50% TOG)
