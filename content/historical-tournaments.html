This database of historical tournaments is a comprehensive list of all Draftstars contests dating back to September 2020. The database is updated daily to include new contests and report the winners. The original Draftstars tournament page can be accessed by clicking on the respective tournament name.

Optimal lineups are available for completed AFL contests (other sports will follow in future) by clicking on the optimal lineup hyperlink.
