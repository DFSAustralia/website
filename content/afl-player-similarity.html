Player Similarity Index is a measure of how similar two players statistical averages over the previous 10 games are, a value of 100 would indicate players are identical. To calculate the similarity index 16 different statistical categories are used, these include but are not limited to disposals, marks, contested vs uncontested, centre bounce attendances, intercepts, inside 50s, rebound 50s, etc.
